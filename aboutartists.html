Owen Payette McGarry, BFA, of Somerville, MA and Sophie Griswold, BS, PHD Student, of Boston, MA have been artistic collaborators for over 12 years. Their diverse œuvre includes: a previous text based videogame called Bike Courier Pro, an avant garde electronic band Ethyl Keytone and the Carboxyl Group, a series of papier-mâché headdresses, and an unpublished collection of short stories titled A Single Raisined Eye.
